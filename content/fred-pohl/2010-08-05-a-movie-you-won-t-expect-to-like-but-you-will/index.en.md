## A Movie You Won’t Expect to Like, But You Will

{{< youtube qnm885kLggY >}}

It’s called [The Messenger](https://web.archive.org/web/20140912000236/http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0036RPM8Y?ie=UTF8&tag=twtfb-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B0036RPM8Y); it stars Woody Harrelson and it’s the story of a lifer Army captain and a used-up, shot-over staff sergeant who get assigned to the stressful job of bringing to GI families the news that their son, father or husband has just been killed in Afghanistan.


I never heard of anyone in the cast but Harrelson and expected little from the film.  But it really got to me.  I think it would to you, too.
