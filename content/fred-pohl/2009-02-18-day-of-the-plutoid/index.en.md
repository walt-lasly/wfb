## Day of the Plutoid

![Pluto](pluto.jpg)

Today is the anniversary of the day in 1930 when young [Clyde Tombaugh](https://web.archive.org/web/20090303053845/http://www.epcc.edu/nwlibrary/borderlands/25/tombaugh.htm), blink-comparing a bunch of astronomical photographs, found a dot that had moved — [Pluto](https://web.archive.org/web/20090303053845/http://www.sciencenews.org/view/generic/id/33158/title/From_planet_to_plutoid) — and told the world he had discovered a new planet.  Boy!  What some people won’t say to make themselves look important!
