## You are there: Frederik Pohl at Windycon 39


{{< figure src="5216748" alt="Frederik Pohl (Photo by Leigh T. Hanlon)." caption="Frederik Pohl at Windycon 39, Nov. 10, 2012 (Photo by Leigh T. Hanlon)." >}}


Fred made a rare live appearance last month at Windycon in Lombard, Ill., talking about his 75-year career. Leigh Hanlon of [ChicagoScope](https://web.archive.org/web/20140917210125/http://chicagoscope.com/frederik-pohl-presentation-at-windycon-39) recorded it, so you can hear it, too.


[Elizabeth Anne Hull](https://web.archive.org/web/20140917210125/http://www.thewaythefutureblogs.com/elizabeth-anne-hull/) also features, and blogmaster [Leah A. Zeldes](https://web.archive.org/web/20140917210125/http://www.zeldes.com/) is the interviewer.
