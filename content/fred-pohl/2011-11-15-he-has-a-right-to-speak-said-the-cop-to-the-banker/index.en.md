## ‘He has a right to speak,’said the cop to the banker


![Move your money](moveyourmoney2.jpg)


My friend Sherry Gottlieb, who used to run the last surviving science-fiction bookstore in L.A. until she just couldn’t keep up with the escalating rents, sent me [the story behind this flyer](https://web.archive.org/web/20111121083259/http://www.dailykos.com/story/2011/11/02/1032624/-He-has-a-right-to-speak,-said-the-cop-to-the-banker).  I love it.  Makes me proud to be an American.  I hope you are the same.

[‘He has a right to speak,’said the cop to the banker
by marvinborg](https://web.archive.org/web/20111121083259/http://www.dailykos.com/story/2011/11/02/1032624/-He-has-a-right-to-speak,-said-the-cop-to-the-banker)
> Like most bullies, the banks are cowards. They talk a big game, but if confronted with their crimes, they run for cover and go whining to “mommy.”

> Today, I walked up and down a sidewalk, in front of a branch of Chase and a branch of BofA. I handed out about 250 flyers during lunch hour.

> They panicked and called their private security people, then more private security and finally the cops. That’s when they found out that they didn’t have a leg to stand on.
