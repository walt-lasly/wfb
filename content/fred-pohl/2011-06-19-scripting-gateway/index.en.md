## ScriptingGateway


{{< figure src="frederik-pohl-iv-head.jpg" alt="Frederik Pohl IV" caption="Frederik Pohl IV" >}}


I used the word “movie” in conjunction with the word “[Gateway](https://web.archive.org/web/20170620030516/http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0345475836?ie=UTF8&tag=7159-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=0345475836)” the [**other day**](/fred-pohl/2011-05-15-sigfrid-von-shrink-builders-wanted/), and several quick-witted blog readers wrote in to ask what was going to happen with a film for my novel of that title.  I shouldn’t have said anything, because it’s a long way from anything tangible, but I was kind of excited about it.


The small amount of news behind my remark is just that, after the previous producers spent somewhere around a million dollars on scripts without finding one they could film, I decided, in collaboration with my son Rick, aka Fred the 4th, to write our own.  I do hope it works out well and maybe earns us at least an Emmy or something.


Rick already has three Emmys of his own, but his problem is his [good wife](https://web.archive.org/web/20170620030516/http://www.cbspressexpress.com/div.php/paramount_network/release?id=19344) is collecting them faster than he is, so what he would really like is an Oscar.


Of course, before we can start thinking about that we really should finish writing the script.
