## Si Nga PlltxeUniltìrantokx?(Do You SpeakAvatar?)

{{< youtube lYBKCIcqa50 >}}

All the time some of us put in to learn [Klingon](https://web.archive.org/web/20140902175226/http://www.kli.org/) no longer avails, because the new *lingua faana* is the one  [devised](https://web.archive.org/web/20140902175226/http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/herocomplex/2009/11/usc-professor-creates-alien-language-for-avatar.html) by linguistics professor [Paul Frommer](https://web.archive.org/web/20140902175226/http://marshallapps.usc.edu/portal/subapps/digitalmeasures/faculty.jsp?surveyId=48767) for James Cameron’s [Avatar](https://web.archive.org/web/20140902175226/http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B002VPE1B6?ie=UTF8&tag=twtfb-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=390957&creativeASIN=B002VPE1B6). With an existing [vocabulary](https://web.archive.org/web/20140902175226/http://www.navilator.com/) of hundreds of words and using sounds that are familiar in terrestrial languages — but not all of them in English — it sounds musical and plausibly foreign.


[Language lessons](https://web.archive.org/web/20140902175226/http://www.learnnavi.org/) at your next con?
