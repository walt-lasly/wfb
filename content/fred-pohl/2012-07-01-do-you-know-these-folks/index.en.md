## Do you know these folks?


{{< figure src="jkk_unknown_b.jpg" alt="Unknown #1 (Photo by Jay Kay Klein)." caption="Unknown #1 (Photo by Jay Kay Klein)." >}}


As I mentioned yesterday, the late [**Jay Kay Klein**](/posts/2012-06-30-so-long-jay-kay-cons-won-t-be-the-same-without-you/) gave me permission to use his photos and sent me a box of them before he died.


Unfortunately, not all of them are captioned. I’ve been sorting through them, and trying to put names to the faces. Here are two of them, and if anyone can identify the people in them, please let me know.
