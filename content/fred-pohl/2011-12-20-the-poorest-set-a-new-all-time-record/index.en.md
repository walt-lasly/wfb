## The Poorest Set a New All-Time Record!

![eat the rich](eat-the-rich.jpg)

Think you’re poor just because your annual income is below the poverty level? What about the people with less than half that?  Why, they’re the [fastest-growing class](https://web.archive.org/web/20170717164420/http://www.theatlanticwire.com/national/2011/11/there-are-more-poorer-americans-nows/44487/) in America, encompassing some 20.5 million people, or 6.7 percent of all Americans.


That’s an all-time record.  But keep going, Republican House of Representatives and Supreme Court.  You can get that number bigger!
