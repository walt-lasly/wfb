## Einstein vs. God: Who’s Ahead?


{{< figure src="albert-einstein-1920.jpg" alt="Albert Einstein, 1920." caption="Albert Einstein, 1920." >}}


[Science](https://web.archive.org/web/20150923001818/http://www.sciencemag.org/) published this extract  from an article called “[Counterexamples to Relativity](https://web.archive.org/web/20150923001818/http://conservapedia.com/Counterexamples_to_Relativity)”  from Conservapedia, which calls itself “an encyclopedia written from a conservative viewpoint”:


“The theory of relativity … is heavily promoted by liberals who like its encouragement of  relativitism and its tendency to mislead people in how they view the world. . . . Virtually no one who is taught and believes relativity continues to read the Bible.”
