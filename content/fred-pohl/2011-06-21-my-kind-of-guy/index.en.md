## My Kind of Guy


{{< figure src="sidney-glassberg-235x300.jpg" alt="Sidney Glassberg" caption="Sidney Glassberg" >}}


A 90-year-old person named [Sidney Glassberg](https://web.archive.org/web/20120826232912/http://politicalmavens.com/index.php/2011/03/30/sidney-glassberg-2/) died in Chicago this spring, and although I never met him I love him.  He did what all of us should try to do: ease our dying for the ones we leave behind by making a joke of it.  According to the death notice in the paper, he left instructions for his hearse to be followed closely by a Brinks truck bearing a sign that said —


*“Who Says You Can’t Take It With You?”*


God, if any, bless you, Sidney, wherever you are now.


And as the nurse came into the hospital room and asked him,  “Are you comfortable?”  he shrugged and said,  “I make a living.”


Hardly anybody ever heard of Sidney Glassberg while he was with us, but he brightened the world for the rest of us as he left.
