## Vacancies


![Lincolnwood, IL](36698930_226f5daa9f.jpg)

  

“The Purple Hyatt,”  once home to Chicago’s [Capricons](https://web.archive.org/web/20170718040004/http://capricon.org/) and other fan gatherings, may soon be just a lavender memory. Legal and financial maneuverings have spared the building’s life until now, but the village of  Lincolnwood has had the legal right to [tear it down](https://web.archive.org/web/20170718040004/http://www.suntimes.com/business/roeder/7102998-452/sssss.html) since Aug. 1.  It’s still standing, empty, but unlikely ever to have anyone live there again because of its numerous violations.


And now it has a sister hotel, also sometimes used for fan gatherings, including Capricon: the onetime Arlington Park Hilton, next to the Arlington Race Course in Arlington Heights. Emptied out and locked up more than a year ago, it’s in better shape than the Purple one, but just as empty and with an equally [unclear future](https://web.archive.org/web/20170718040004/http://dhbusinessledger.com/main.asp?SubSectionID=29&ArticleID=2772&SectionID=4).
