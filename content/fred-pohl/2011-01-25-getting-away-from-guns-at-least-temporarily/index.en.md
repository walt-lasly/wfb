## Getting Away from Guns (At Least Temporarily)


![No guns allowed](no-guns.png)


The comments in response to [**the post on the shooting of the Congresswoman**](/fred-pohl/2011-01-13-mass-murder-courtesy-of-the-nra/) were pretty vigorous, so we left it up top for a week to make sure everybody got his say.  I think there’s a lot to be learned from the discussion, and so I’ll try to come back to it soon.


Meanwhile, though, I think two things, first that we’re beginning to repeat ourselves a bit more than we like, and second that it’s about time we got back to the mix of topics that most of you seem to prefer.


And so—


This Correspondence Is Closed
