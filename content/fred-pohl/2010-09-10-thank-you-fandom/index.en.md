# The Way the Future Blogs


Frederik Pohl


## Thank You, Fandom!


{{< figure src="hugo.jpg" alt="My new Hugo. (Photo courtesy Helen Montgomery.)" caption="My new Hugo. (Photo courtesy Helen Montgomery.)" >}}


To say that I’m pleased to have won [**this Hugo**](/fred-pohl/2010-09-05-news-from-aussiecon/) doesn’t really cover the subject, because I’m not just pleased, I’m tickled pink.  So I want to thank everybody who voted in this year’s event, whoever they voted for; to thank as well all the nice people, beloved friends as well as total strangers, who sent me messages of congratulation and affection; and finally to thank the whole world of science-fiction fandom, which I have inhabited since before I quite reached my teens, and to which I will stop giving my allegiance when I stop breathing, but not before.
