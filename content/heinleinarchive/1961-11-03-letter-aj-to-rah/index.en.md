# Letter from Algis Budrys to RAH

*A brief follow-up note written the day after AJ's long letter of November 2. He realized he had written at length about the enclosed family photographs — and then forgotten to enclose them. The handwritten postscript jokes about the group photo.*

{{< figure src="regency_logo.png" >}}

{{< typewriter >}}

<p class="letter-date">November 3, 1961</p>

Dear Bob:

Goes to show what extreme fatigue can do to the mental process. I wrote you yesterday (surface mail) and devoted quite a few words to the pictures enclosed.

Didn't enclose pictures.

bests,

{{< figure src="signature_aj.png" alt="AJ" caption="AJ" >}}


✲ ✲ ✲

<p class="handwritten"><em>P.S.</em> I'm not exactly sure why Tim's unhappy, in the group picture, but I think the look on Steve's face, along with the fact we can't see both of Steve's hands is evidence of something or other.</p>

{{< /typewriter >}}

---

**Notes**

**Tim, Steve, Jeff** — AJ's first three sons, described in detail in the November 2 letter: Jeff (eldest, tree-climbing acrobat), Steve (curly, blue-eyed, introverted), and Tim (dark-haired, Irish-tempered). A fourth son, David, would arrive in January 1962.
