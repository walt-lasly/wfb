## Vacancies


![Lincolnwood, IL](36698930_226f5daa9f.jpg)

  

“The Purple Hyatt,”  once home to Chicago’s Capricons and other fan gatherings, may soon be just a lavender memory. Legal and financial maneuverings have spared the building’s life until now, but the village of  Lincolnwood has had the legal right to tear it down since Aug. 1.  It’s still standing, empty, but unlikely ever to have anyone live there again because of its numerous violations.


And now it has a sister hotel, also sometimes used for fan gatherings, including Capricon: the onetime Arlington Park Hilton, next to the Arlington Race Course in Arlington Heights. Emptied out and locked up more than a year ago, it’s in better shape than the Purple one, but just as empty and with an equally unclear future.
