## Little Known Fun Facts


**IQ, Do U?**


{{< figure src="lterman.gif" alt="Lewis Terman" caption="Lewis Terman" >}}


Remember the Terman kids?  The high-IQ teenagers [Lewis Terman](https://web.archive.org/web/20090922055733/http://www.indiana.edu/~intell/terman.shtml) collected in the 1920s to follow through their adult lives to see whether getting high marks on the test (their average IQ was 150) really did mean intellectual success as a grownup?  In the end, two of his candidates wound up as Nobel laureates, but they didn’t show up on his results.  Terman had dropped them from his study. Their IQs weren’t high enough.


**If It Hurts, Talk Dirty**


Psychologist [Richard Stephens](https://web.archive.org/web/20090922055733/http://www.keele.ac.uk/depts/ps/people/RStephens/index.htm) (UK’s Keele University), working with volunteers, applied pain stimuli to them and told them their audible response would be recorded.  He instructed some, randomly selected, to avoid bad language while the rest were permitted to let the censorable words rip.  On analysis of the results, the foul-mouthed sufferers turned out to be able to handle pain better than the prissy ones.
