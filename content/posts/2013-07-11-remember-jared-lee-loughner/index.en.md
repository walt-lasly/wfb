## Remember Jared Lee Loughner?


{{< figure src="Gabrielle-Giffords.jpg" alt="Gabrielle Giffords" caption="Gabrielle Giffords" >}}


You know, the guy that shot up a bunch of citizens who were peacefully listening to their congresswoman, Gabrielle Giffords?  Laughner apparently was hoping to assassinate Ms Giffords, probably because she was a Democrat and had the temerity to be one in Arizona.  He came pretty close, too.  He even put a bullet in her brain, but she fooled him and recovered.  He did kill six other people, for no particular reason, just because they got in the way of the bullets from his Glock.


Anyway, when they started to try him for his murderous ways, they couldn’t.  He spouted gibberish, struggled with the U.S. marshals holding him and didn’t seem to understand what judges or jurors were for.


It would seem to me that if someone is too nutty to be tried for his murders, he’s way too nutty to be allowed to buy a gun in the first place.  Am I missing something important here?
